How to Register and Login

1. Register a New Account
Go to the Register page. Fill in your email, password, and name, then click Register. If successful, you'll see a confirmation message.
2. Login
Go to the Login page. Enter the email and password you registered with, then click Sign In. If successful, you'll be logged in and can access the portal.
3. Troubleshooting
If you see an error, double-check your email and password. Make sure you registered first. If you still have issues, contact support.
